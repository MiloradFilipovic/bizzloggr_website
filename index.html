BizzLoggr

BizzLoggr is a mobile app that can identify and show all business places you called but don't have saved in your contacts. It can help you keep track of your interactions and enables you to organize identified places just the way you want it.

Start loggn' now!

What can BizzLoggr do for me?

  • Do you have to search online each time you want to call a nice restaurant?
  • Maybe you don't have your dentist's number saved or you forgot when was the last appointment?
  • Are you often in touch with business partners or clients? Would it be nice to have their full info together with your interaction history?
  • BizzLoggr will show information about all unknown numbers in your call history it can identify.
  • You can access your chosen places faster by adding them to favorites.
  • Save place to your contacts with the provided data filled in for you.
  • View selected place on a map so you can easily navigate to it's address if needed.
