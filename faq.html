Frequently asked questions

About BizzLoggr

Can you describe Bizzloggr in a few sentences for me?

It's a mobile app that will scan your phone's call log and identify all business numbers you don't have in your contacts. You can use it to see places and businesses you occasionally interact with but not often enough to remember to save them in contacts, to find out time or frequency of your calls to a particular place, or just if you are curious and want to see what your call log has become over the years. It can also help you better organize identified places and provide some useful features like showing place on map or adding it to contacts with the gathered data already set up.

Who is this app for?

Bizzloggr can be useful to anyone who makes calls with their phone. It is perfect if you don't have the habit to add random places you contact occasionally to your phonebook. It can help you find a certain place you contacted a while ago, or to remember when was the last time you called it. Once you have the places identified, you can use and arrange them the way you want. For business people, it can provide valuable information about your business clients and your interaction history with them.

What kind of phone numbers can Bizzloggr identify?

Bizzloggr is able to identify business numbers that are registered on map services like Google maps or Bing maps. It is not intended to be used for personal numbers unless they are associated with a place on a map. Places data is usually provided by general public users or business owners and it's accuracy may vary.

Why are there two versions of this app?

Bizzloggr Lite is a free version intended to give you a sneak peak of what the app looks and works like before you decide to buy it. However, it is only able to process calls made during the last month and it lacks additional features like favorite places or detailed call history for a selected place. Bizzloggr is the full version of this app which provides the complete Bizzloggr experience including unlimited call history and all of the features which help you get a better overview and organize your places to your liking. We will keep improving the app by adding more useful stuff in the future updates for the full version, while Bizzloggr Lite will receive only the most crucial ones.

Why do I have to pay for the full version?

Good question. First of all, the data provided by the app is not free to get. Service providers we use will charge to identify each number in you call log and the app price is estimated based on that costs. Regardless, making such a nice app takes time and effort and it always good to support the developers for providing awesome solutions and experiences.

Privacy and security

Should I feel safe giving permissions to this app on my phone?

In order to identify the numbers Bizzloggr has to access your phone's call log so this permission is a part of the core functionality of the app. We are committed to keep minimal permission requirements and take care of your private data so, other than using phone numbers from your log to get the place information, the app is not using call history data in any other way. Other than that, the app needs the permission to access the internet so it can fetch place data for each call. So, as you can see, Bizzloggr only ask you to allow it to do it's job: List your calls and try to identify them. Nothing more.

What happens to my private data the app has access to?

Nothing. All data about calls you made stays with you. Bizzloggr app is not storing it in our database or using it in any other way than intended. Please read our Privacy policy to get the detailed understanding about the way we treat your private information.

Troubleshooting

I got no results, help!

First of all, make sure you have a working and stable internet connection. If that's the case, try uninstalling the app and installing it again (don't worry, you won't be charged again if you already purchased the app). In some cases, Bizzloggr will not be able to identify the numbers made by your previous SIM card(s) especially if they were not from your current operator or country. In the end, maybe you just don't have any identifiable numbers in your call log.

Why are some places gone from my list?

Bizzloggr is always processing calls from your phone's call log, so if you have deleted a call in your default dialing app, the associated place will also disappear from Bizzloggr place list if there are not other calls to it.
